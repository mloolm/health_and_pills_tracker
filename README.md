# Health&Pills

Health&Pills is a privacy-focused personal medication and wellness tracker.

The app helps users:

* track medication schedules
* receive medication reminders
* monitor vital signs and health metrics
* manage doctor visits and health-related events
* track menstrual cycles
* review statistics and trends over time

## Privacy First

Health&Pills is designed to work primarily offline.

* no advertising
* no analytics SDKs
* no tracking
* no external servers for personal health data

All user data is stored locally on the device unless the user explicitly exports a backup.

## Health Connect

The app can optionally integrate with Health Connect on Android to read selected health data such as:

* heart rate
* blood oxygen (SpO₂)
* weight

This integration is optional and can be disabled at any time.

## Medical Disclaimer

Health&Pills is intended for informational and personal tracking purposes only.

The app does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
