Petly — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

1. Overview

Petly ("the App") helps you track pet vaccinations, treatments, feeding, and reminders. The App is designed to keep your pet records on your device. This policy explains what data the App handles and how.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

The App does not:

  • collect, transmit, or upload pet records to our server
  • collect human medical or health data
  • use analytics SDKs
  • track your in-app behavior for our own analytics
  • sell your data

3. Data Stored on Your Device

Information you enter — pet profiles, vaccinations, treatments, feeding plans, reminders, notes, and settings — is stored locally on your device only, in the App's private database. This data is not uploaded to us and is not shared with third parties by the App.

4. Advertising

The free version of the App may show ads. Ads can be removed with an optional in-app purchase.

Ads may be provided by third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob. These services may process limited device or advertising data, such as advertising identifiers, approximate location, device information, ad interactions, and diagnostic data, to show ads, measure performance, and prevent fraud. We do not send your pet records, reminders, notes, or feeding data to advertising services.

You can manage ad personalization in your device or Google settings where available.

5. Purchases

If you buy the optional ad removal purchase, the purchase is processed by Google Play or the Apple App Store. We do not receive your payment card details.

6. Notifications

The App may send local notifications for pet care reminders. These notifications are generated on your device and do not require sending your pet records to any server.

7. Backup

If the App offers backup or export features, any backup file is created on your device and stored only where you choose to save or share it. We have no access to your backup files.

8. Children

The App does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
