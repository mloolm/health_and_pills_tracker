Health&Pills — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Overview

Health&Pills ("the App") is a personal medication and health tracker designed to run entirely on your device. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App handles and how.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

The App does not:

  • Collect, transmit, or upload any personal or health data to any server
  • use analytics SDKs or advertising networks
  • share any data with third parties
  • track your usage or behavior

3. Data Stored on Your Device

All information you enter — medications, schedules, vital readings, and settings — is stored locally on your device only, in a private database that is not accessible to other apps. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use the backup/export feature.

4. Health Connect / Apple Health (Optional)

If you choose to connect the App to Health Connect (Android) or Apple Health (iOS), the App will read health data (heart rate, blood oxygen, weight) from your device only. This data is displayed within the App and stored in the local database. It is never sent anywhere. You can revoke this access at any time in your device's Health settings.

5. Notifications

The App may send local notifications as medication reminders. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external service.

6. Backup

The App includes an optional backup feature that lets you export your data to a file on your device. This file is stored only where you choose to save it. We have no access to it.

7. Children

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
